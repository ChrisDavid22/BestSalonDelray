Sun Protection Strategies

Daily Protection

  • UV protection spray (SPF 30+)

  • Avoid peak sun (11am-3pm)

Color Protection

  • Color-safe shampoo essential
  • Purple shampoo for blondes weekly
  • Gloss treatment every 6 weeks
  • Cool water rinses only

Repair Treatments

  • Weekly deep conditioning mask
  • Monthly Olaplex treatment

  • Trim every 6-8 weeks
