Keratin Treatments in Delray Beach: Your Guide to Frizz-Free Hair

Everything you need to know about smoothing treatments in South Florida's humidity

Expert Guide | Updated December 2025

Why Keratin is Popular in Florida: With 70-80% humidity most of the year, South Florida is ground zero for frizzy hair. Keratin treatments smooth the hair cuticle, eliminating frizz for 3-6 months regardless of weather.

What is a Keratin Treatment?

Keratin treatments (also called Brazilian blowouts, smoothing treatments, or hair relaxing treatments) use keratin protein to fill gaps in the hair cuticle, creating a smooth, frizz-free finish.

The treatment is applied, sealed with heat, and results last 3-6 months depending on the formula and your hair care routine.

Finding a Certified Keratin Specialist

Keratin treatments require proper training to apply safely and effectively. Look for:

Brand Certification

Certified in specific treatments (Cezanne, Brazilian Blowout, etc.). Each brand has different application techniques.

Proper Ventilation

The salon should have proper ventilation systems. Fumes from some treatments require air circulation.

Color Experience

If you have colored hair, make sure the stylist understands how keratin interacts with different color treatments.
